    Website project

    Gizzi Dental Clinic

    Objetive

    Develop a comprehensive digital platform that increases the visibility of the Gizzi Clinic, facilitates access to medical information and services for patients, improve administrative efficiency through online appointment scheduling and documentation management, and strengthen the relationship with current and potential patients through educational resources and communication channels interactive.



    Phase: Planning and Analysis


    In the planning and analysis phase, the objectives are to define the objectives and requirements of the project, as well as identifying the needs and expectations of users, which include the patients and clinic staff. Tasks include an initial meeting to discuss the objectives, scope and expectations of the project, and establish a preliminary schedule with assigned responsibilities. A requirements analysis is carried out by collecting information about the services, specialties and unique characteristics of the clinic, in addition to identifying the needs and preferences of current and potential patients through surveys or interviews. I also know performs competitive research by analyzing the websites of other clinics and competitors to Identify good practices and opportunities for improvement. Finally, the scope of the project is defined creating a detailed requirements document that includes all functionalities and features desired from the web page.
