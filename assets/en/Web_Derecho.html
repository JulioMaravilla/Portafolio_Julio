    Website project

    Student Law

    objective

    The Student Rights website's main objective is to become a comprehensive and accessible resource for all students, promoting an understanding deep and active defense of their rights in the educational environment. This platform seeks to empower students with the knowledge and tools necessary to ensure that their rights are respected and protected, thus fostering an environment equitable, safe and fair schooling for all.



    Phase: Development and Implementation


    The development phase of the student rights website begins with a meeting initial stakeholder consultation to define the purpose and scope of the project, followed by a needs analysis to identify specific content requirements and functionalities. A detailed project plan is prepared, establishing schedules and resource allocation. The interface design focuses on usability and accessibility, while technical development ensures a robust structure and safe. During development, content is created and organized, including guides, practical resources and tools, and interactive functionalities such as forums are integrated and contact systems.
